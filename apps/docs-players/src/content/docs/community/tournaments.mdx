## Tournament Overview

Test your skills in organized competitive play and win exclusive rewards.

## Tournament Types

### Weekly Tournaments

- **When:** Every Saturday
- **Format:** Best of 3, Swiss rounds
- **Entry:** Free
- **Prizes:** Gold, packs, exclusive cards

### Monthly Championships

- **When:** Last weekend of each month
- **Format:** Best of 3, Top 8 elimination
- **Entry:** Qualify through weekly events
- **Prizes:** Exclusive card backs, titles

### Seasonal Invitational

- **When:** End of each season
- **Format:** Best of 5, Double elimination
- **Entry:** Top 100 ranked players
- **Prizes:** Cash prizes, exclusive content

## Tournament Rules

1. **Deck submission** - Submit your deck before the event
2. **No deck changes** - Use the same deck throughout
3. **Sideboards** - 10-card sideboard allowed (Best of 3+)
4. **Time limits** - 25 minutes per match

## How to Qualify

1. **Play Ranked** - High ranked players get invites
2. **Win Weekly Events** - Top finishers qualify for monthlies
3. **Community Events** - Partner tournaments offer qualification
