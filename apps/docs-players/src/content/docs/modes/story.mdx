## Story Mode Overview

Journey through the world of Aethermoor in an epic single-player campaign.

## Chapters

### Act I: Awakening

Begin your journey as a new summoner learning to harness elemental power.

- **Chapter 1:** The Call
- **Chapter 2:** First Flames
- **Chapter 3:** Trial of Elements

### Act II: The Gathering Storm

Join the conflict between factions as war threatens Aethermoor.

- **Chapter 4:** Choosing Sides
- **Chapter 5:** The Frost March
- **Chapter 6:** Shadows Rising

### Act III: The Final Battle

Face the ultimate threat and decide the fate of the realm.

- **Chapter 7:** United Front
- **Chapter 8:** The Void Gate
- **Chapter 9:** Destiny's End

## Rewards

Completing story chapters unlocks:

- New cards
- Alternate art versions
- Lore entries
- Character backstories
- Exclusive card backs
