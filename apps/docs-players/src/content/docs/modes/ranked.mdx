## Earning Tier Points

| Result | Tier Points |
|--------|-------------|
| Win | +25 to +35 TP |
| Loss | -20 to -25 TP |
| Win Streak (3+) | +5 bonus TP |

<Aside type="tip">
  Win streaks give bonus TP! Three wins in a row starts the streak.
</Aside>

## Season Structure

- Each season lasts **3 months**
- Ranks reset at season end (soft reset)
- Season rewards based on highest rank achieved

### Season Rewards

| Rank Achieved | Reward |
|---------------|--------|
| Gold+ | Exclusive card back |
| Diamond+ | Exclusive card + card back |
| Master+ | Legendary card + exclusive title |
| Legend | All rewards + special border |

## Tips for Climbing

1. **Play your best deck** - Don't experiment in ranked
2. **Learn matchups** - Know how to play vs common decks
3. **Take breaks** - Stop after 2-3 losses in a row
4. **Track your stats** - Identify and fix weaknesses
5. **Play at off-peak times** - Often easier opponents
