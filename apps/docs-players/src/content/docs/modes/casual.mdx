## Casual Overview

Casual mode lets you play without affecting your ranked standing. Perfect for:

- Testing new decks
- Learning the game
- Playing with friends
- Completing daily quests

## Features

- **No rank changes** - Win or lose, your rank is safe
- **Deck experimentation** - Try weird combos
- **Relaxed atmosphere** - No pressure to win
- **Quest completion** - Most quests work in casual

## When to Play Casual

| Situation | Casual? |
|-----------|---------|
| Testing a new deck | ✅ Yes |
| Completing quests | ✅ Yes |
| Warming up | ✅ Yes |
| Serious climbing | ❌ Play Ranked |
| Learning a matchup | ✅ Yes |
