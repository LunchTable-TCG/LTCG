## Quick Start

1. **Understand the Basics** - Each player starts with 20 Life Points and 5 Mana per turn
2. **Build a Deck** - Create a 40-60 card deck with creatures, spells, and traps
3. **Battle** - Summon creatures, cast spells, and reduce your opponent to 0 LP
4. **Win** - Be the last player standing or force a deck-out
